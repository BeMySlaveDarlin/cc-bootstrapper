# Privacy Policy

cc-bootstrapper operates entirely locally on your machine.

- **No data collection** — the plugin does not collect, transmit, or store any user data
- **No network requests** — all operations are performed locally using your filesystem
- **No telemetry** — no analytics, tracking, or usage reporting
- **No external services** — the plugin does not connect to any remote servers

All generated files (agents, pipelines, skills, hooks, settings) are written to your project's `.claude/` directory and remain under your full control.
